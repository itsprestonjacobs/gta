About SFRP

Welcome to San Fierro Roleplay, a FiveM server focused on immersive roleplay set in the iconic San Fierro city of GTA: San Andreas. Join us for a unique roleplaying experience, with active moderators, custom jobs, and an engaging storyline!

How to Join

To join our server, follow these steps:

  1. Install FiveM
  2. Search for SFRP in the server list
  3. Click Join and enjoy!

Server Rules

  • Respect all players and staff members.
  • No cheating or exploiting bugs.
  • Keep the roleplay realistic and immersive.
  • Follow the server-specific guidelines listed on our forums.
